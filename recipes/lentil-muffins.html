Lentil Muffins

Description

These lentil muffins are a great way to increase the nutritional content of your grab-and-go breakfast. The addition of lentils brings extra protein and fiber to every bite. With the addition of banana, these muffins also pack all of the flavor of your traditional muffin.

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Bananas
  • Canola Oil
  • Egg
  • Refined White Sugar
  • Rolled Oats
  • White Whole Wheat Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Optional: Chocolate Chips
    •

    Instructions

    1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
    2. Use a food processor to blend lentils, oil, egg, banana, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
    3. Mix in, but don't blend, the oats, flour, baking soda, and cinnamon.
    4. Add chocolate chips if desired.
    5. Scoop into greased muffin tin and bake for 18 minutes. Test with toothpick.
    6. Enjoy!
